# Pricing & Packages

Sell output, not time. Price for buyers who understand CAC and will deploy the creative in paid spend.

---

## Packages

| Package | Deliverables | Price |
|---------|-------------|-------|
| **UGC Video Pack** | 20 videos/mo (3 angles, hook A/B/C variations, cold + retargeting mix) | $1,500/mo |
| **UGC + Photos** | 20 videos + 10 statics/product photos | $2,000/mo |
| **Full Retainer** | 40 videos + monthly A/B concept strategy + launch sheets + iteration on winners | $3,000/mo |

Every package includes: angle research doc, dedicated Soul Character, launch sheet, one revision round per batch.

## Pricing rules

- **Floor is $1,500.** $300 packs attract clients who treat this like Canva templates, demand infinite revisions, and never run paid spend — so you get no performance data and no case study. $1,500+ filters for businesses that measure ROAS.
- **Anchor to what it replaces:** a single human UGC creator charges $150–$300 *per video*; an agency retainer for 20 videos runs $5–10K. You're 50–80% cheaper with faster turnaround — say so.
- **Price the strategy, not the render.** The pitch is "20 tested angles/month," never "AI videos." If the client hears "AI videos," they price-compare against the generation cost.

## Who to sell to

**Primary: existing ModernFlow clients + local service businesses.** Already trust us, already run local ads, zero acquisition cost. Auto repair, salons, clinics, restaurants, contractors — everyone needs Reels and none of them can film consistently.

**Secondary: Shopify DTC brands doing $100K–$2M/yr.** Big enough to spend on Meta, small enough to have no in-house creative team, and bleeding from ad fatigue. They don't need banger creative — they need 20 fresh hooks per month to keep ROAS alive. Skip the giants (procurement, brand police) and the pre-revenue startups (no ad spend = no results = churn).

**Qualifying question:** "What's your current monthly ad spend?" Under ~$3K/mo, they can't consume 20 videos and won't see the value. Refer them to the base ModernFlow automation package instead.

## Other verticals worth pitching (same system, different Soul)

- Amazon FBA/FBM — listing creatives + ad videos
- Fitness coaches — transformation-style content, workout clips
- Restaurants/F&B — menu showcases, "day in the kitchen"
- Course creators — ad creatives, testimonial-style clips (disclosure rules apply, see doc 07)
- SaaS / app developers — UGC-style feature previews
- Real estate — listing videos, neighborhood spotlights
- Event promotion — speaker hype, recap teasers

Avoid regulated/reputational minefields (health claims, financial products, political content) until there's a compliance process in place — one ad-account ban costs more than the retainer.

## Time budget per client (target)

~5–6 hours/month at steady state: 1h brief/check-in, 1.5h angle research + curation, 2h generation supervision + QC, 1h delivery + launch sheet. If a client consistently takes 10+ hours, the strategy doc approval step is being skipped — fix the process, not the price.
