# Company

## Company name
ModernFlow AI

## What we do
We build done-for-you AI automation systems for home-service contractors so no lead ever goes unanswered. We install the CRM, the AI follow-up, and the automations — the contractor just runs their business from their phone.

## Core offer
A finished, working lead-to-booked-job system, built in 2–3 weeks:

- **Custom CRM** (built on GoHighLevel): automated lead capture and tagging, visual deal pipeline, contact history, mobile-friendly dashboard
- **AI Follow-Up Assistants**: respond to leads in under 5 minutes via SMS, email, and webchat — 24/7; qualify leads, answer questions, book appointments
- **Automation Engine**: lead nurture sequences, appointment reminders, post-job review requests, re-engagement campaigns, missed-call auto-text
- **Paid Ads Management** (optional add-on): Google Local Service Ads, Facebook/Instagram campaigns, landing page optimization, monthly performance reports
