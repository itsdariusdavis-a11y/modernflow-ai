# Template: Prospect Qualification (Contractor)

Run before any outreach and before every strategy call. The point is to find contractors whose follow-up problem is visible from the outside — and to skip the ones who aren't a fit.

---

Research this potential customer for ModernFlow AI.

COMPANY:
[Name, website, Google Business Profile link]

PERSON:
[Owner name and role, if known]

OUR OFFER:
Done-for-you lead follow-up system for home-service contractors: CRM, AI assistants answering in under 5 minutes 24/7, booking, review automation. Plans from $1,000 setup + $97/mo. See 01-company.md.

OUR IDEAL CUSTOMER:
See 02-customer.md — home-service trades, owner-led, 1–15 people, already getting leads but losing them to slow follow-up.

Find:

1. What trade they're in and what services they sell
2. Service area and apparent size (crew photos, fleet, years in business)
3. Recent changes: hiring posts, new service lines, new trucks, license changes
4. **Follow-up signals** — the ones that matter for us:
   - Google review volume and recency (few/stale reviews = no review automation)
   - Whether their website has a contact form, chat widget, or online booking
   - Whether they run Google Local Service Ads or Facebook ads
   - Review complaints mentioning "never called back," "no response," "hard to reach"
5. Evidence the problem is costing them now
6. Current alternatives they may use (Angi/HomeAdvisor badges, answering service, existing chat widget)
7. Reasons they may NOT be a fit (see bad-fit list in 02-customer.md)
8. One honest, specific reason to contact them this week

Rate the prospect:

A — strong fit and a current trigger (e.g., hiring + review complaints about responsiveness)
B — reasonable fit, no urgent trigger
C — weak fit or bad fit

Do not draft outreach for a C prospect.

For A or B, create a five-line outreach draft:

- specific observation about THEIR business (a real review, their LSA listing, their hiring post);
- likely implication in their trade's terms (missed calls = jobs booked by whoever answered first);
- the relevant result we provide (every lead answered in under 5 minutes, 24/7);
- one verified proof point from 01-company.md — nothing invented;
- low-pressure question ending in the free strategy call as the next step.

Never invent familiarity, results, or personal details.
Keep the message under 100 words.
Save the brief and draft to 04_Review/prospect-[company]-[YYYY-MM-DD].md.
