# Template: One-Page Proposal

Use after a strategy call with an A or B prospect. Once this works manually a few times, graduate to `06_Skills/proposal-builder/`.

---

Create a one-page proposal using:

- the strategy-call notes (from 00_Inbox);
- the approved pricing in 01-company.md (Starter $1,000 + $97/mo, Growth $2,000 + $297/mo, Elite $3,000 + $697/mo — no other numbers exist);
- relevant verified proof from 01-company.md and 02-customer.md.

Structure:

1. Current situation — in the contractor's own words from the call
2. Desired outcome — jobs booked, not features installed
3. Recommended plan and why (Starter / Growth / Elite)
4. Deliverables — the specific plan's inclusions, nothing extra
5. Timeline — system built and live in 2–3 weeks
6. Investment — setup fee + monthly fee, stated plainly; ads add-on only if discussed, with the 90-day minimum stated
7. Client responsibilities — access to existing website, phone number, review platforms; ~1 hour for training
8. Guarantee — 30-day money-back on the build
9. Specific next step — one action with a date

Mark any missing information clearly as MISSING INFORMATION.
Do not invent proof, pricing, scope, or deadlines.
Do not add discounts.
Save the draft to 04_Review/proposal-[client]-[YYYY-MM-DD].md. Never send it.
