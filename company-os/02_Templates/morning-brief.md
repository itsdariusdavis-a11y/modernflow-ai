# Template: Morning Operator Brief

Run manually every weekday for at least a week, correcting the classifications. Your corrections become the raw material for the `06_Skills/morning-brief/` Skill and, later, a scheduled task.

---

Create my morning operating brief for ModernFlow AI.

Use:

- email from the last 18 hours;
- today's calendar and tomorrow morning (strategy calls need prep — flag them);
- active projects and pipeline in 04-operating-context.md;
- recent files in 00_Inbox;
- unresolved items in 05_Logs.

Output:

# Morning Brief — [Date]

## Needs my attention before noon
Only urgent or consequential items. A prospect reply, a client build blocker, or a commitment at risk qualifies. A newsletter does not.

## Meetings today
For each:
- time;
- purpose;
- preparation needed (for strategy calls: is there a prospect brief in 04_Review? If not, flag it);
- unresolved commitment with this person.

## Waiting for me
Messages, approvals, or decisions blocking progress — including proposals sitting in 04_Review.

## Can wait
Important but not urgent.

## One business priority
The single action most connected to this week's main outcome (from 04-operating-context.md).

## Questions
Anything unclear or conflicting.

Rules:

- keep it under 500 words;
- do not draft replies;
- do not send anything;
- do not move files;
- do not treat newsletters or automated notices as urgent;
- client build deadlines and the 2–3 week promise always rank above internal work.

Save to 03_Outputs/morning-brief-[YYYY-MM-DD].md.
