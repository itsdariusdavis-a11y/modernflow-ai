# Start Here — The AI Agency Launch Kit

Welcome. You just bought the operating system of a real AI automation agency —
not a course. Everything in this download is a working file: SOPs we follow,
workflows we run, prompts we use. Here's the fastest path to your first client.

## What's in your download

Depending on your tier, you'll see some or all of these folders:

| Folder                | What it is                                                                                              | Tier            |
| --------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | --------------- |
| `01-playbook/`        | The business plan, 30-day launch plan, 10 SOPs, and 8 operator prompt templates                         | All tiers       |
| `02-automations/`     | Importable n8n workflows: the cold-email machine and the post-job follow-up system, plus setup runbooks | Toolkit & up    |
| `03-command-center/`  | A single-file web app: MRR dashboard, sprint checklist, pipeline tracker, sales scripts                 | Agency-in-a-Box |

## Week 1: read three files, pick your niche

1. `01-playbook/BUSINESS-PLAN.md` — the whole model: offer, tiers, funnel math.
2. `01-playbook/30-DAY-PLAN.md` — your day-by-day launch sequence.
3. `01-playbook/sops/00-overview.md` — how the nine processes fit together.

Then pick ONE service-business niche (HVAC, roofing, plumbing, electrical…) and
ONE metro area. Every SOP works better narrow.

## Week 2: turn on the machine (Toolkit & up)

1. Follow `02-automations/cold-email-machine/README.md` to import the four n8n
   workflows and connect Apollo + Gmail. The runbook lists every credential you
   need and roughly what a 500-lead campaign costs to run.
2. Read the compliance notes before sending anything. CAN-SPAM is not optional.
3. When a client signs, `02-automations/post-job-followup/` is your first
   deliverable — the README includes a 10-minute-per-client cloning SOP.

## Replace the placeholders

Files reference `you@youragency.com` and `calendly.com/YOUR-LINK` — swap in your
own email, booking link, and business name before anything goes client-facing.

## Rules of the road

- The license (`LICENSE.md`) lets you use everything for your own agency and
  your clients. It doesn't allow reselling or redistributing the kit itself.
- Don't invent track-record claims. Sell the system honestly — "here's exactly
  what we'll set up for you" outperforms fake stats anyway.
