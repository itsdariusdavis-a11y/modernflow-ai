# 30-Day Go-To-Market Plan — AI Agency Launch Kit

Goal for the first 30 days: **first 10 sales and a repeatable content engine**,
on roughly 5–7 hours/week. Everything here assumes the launch checklist
(`LAUNCH-CHECKLIST.md`) is done first — live checkout URLs, working /toolkit page.

All creatives referenced below are pre-written in [`creatives/`](creatives/):
video scripts, X posts and threads, IG carousels, and static ad prompts.

---

## 1. Positioning (use this everywhere, verbatim where possible)

> **"I run a real AI automation agency for contractors. I packaged the actual
> files we run on — SOPs, n8n workflows, prompts — into a kit. It's not a
> course. There are no videos. It's the working system."**

Why this wins: the "AI agency" niche is saturated with $997 course-sellers whose
proof is rented Lamborghinis. The counter-position is _artifact proof_ — showing
the actual files, workflows firing, and real client-facing deliverables on
screen. Every piece of content is a screen recording or screenshot of something
real, which nobody selling vaporware can copy.

**Hard rules (FTC + CLAUDE.md):**

- Never state or imply income results for buyers. No "make $10k/mo with this."
- Only cite real numbers: your actual prices, actual workflow run costs, actual
  time-to-set-up. "This is what I charge clients" is fine and powerful.
- Disclose paid ads as ads (platforms handle this) and keep client PII out of
  every screen recording — demo on the sanitized product files, never live
  client accounts.

## 2. The funnel

```
Short-form video (TikTok / Reels / Shorts)          ← discovery, volume
X posts + threads (build-in-public, automation niche) ← authority, high-intent
Reddit + Facebook groups + Gumroad Discover          ← buyers already searching
        │
        ▼
Link-in-bio → modernflowai.com/toolkit  (all links UTM-tagged)
        │
        ├── Buys ($97 / $297 / $497)  → done, Lemon Squeezy fulfills
        └── Doesn't buy → FREE lead magnet → 4-email sequence → tripwire
```

**Lead magnet (build in week 1, ~1 hour):** give away one real piece of the kit
free — the **Calendly booking-alert n8n workflow + one SOP (00-overview)** as a
$0 Lemon Squeezy product ("The AI Agency Starter File"). Email capture is
automatic. It proves the "actual files" claim better than any ad, and the free
workflow is the least commercially valuable of the four.

**Email sequence (4 emails, write once, runs forever via Lemon Squeezy email or
free MailerLite tier):**

1. Day 0 — deliver the file + how to import it in 5 minutes.
2. Day 2 — "the other 3 workflows" (what the cold-email machine does, with a
   screen recording link).
3. Day 4 — objection email: "why this isn't a course," licensing, refund policy.
4. Day 6 — straight offer + $20 launch coupon on the Toolkit tier, 72-hour expiry.

## 3. Channel playbook

### Short-form video — TikTok + Instagram Reels + YouTube Shorts (primary)

- **One video per day**, same file cross-posted to all three (record once,
  export without watermark, post natively to each).
- Format mix that fits this product: **screen-recording demos** (n8n canvas
  zooming, files opening, the command-center app clicking) with voiceover or
  captions — face optional; talking-head only if comfortable. The product IS
  visual: workflows lighting up node-by-node is inherently watchable.
- 21 ready scripts in `creatives/video-scripts.md`, tagged by pillar:
  - **Proof/demo** (~40%): "watch this workflow score an email reply 1–10"
  - **Contrarian/rant** (~25%): "AI agency courses are a scam. Here's what the
    files actually look like."
  - **Value/teach** (~25%): teach one real SOP step fully, free — the kit is
    "all of this, organized."
  - **Offer** (~10%): direct pitch, only after the account has proof content.
- Batch-record 5–7 videos every Monday (~2 hrs). Post 6–9pm local.
- Handle everything as **@modernflowai** or a personal handle — personal
  converts better in this niche if you'll show face at least sometimes.

### X (secondary, highest buyer intent per follower)

- The n8n/automation/build-in-public community on X actually buys templates.
- **1 post per weekday + 1 long thread per week** (drafts in
  `creatives/x-content.md`). Threads = repurposed video scripts with
  screenshots.
- Spend 15 min/day replying under big automation/AI-agency accounts (n8n,
  GoHighLevel, indie-hacker posters) — replies are the follower engine at zero
  count. Never drop links in replies; link only in your own posts/bio.

### Instagram feed (light)

- **2 carousels per week** (slide-by-slide copy in `creatives/ig-carousels.md`)
  - daily Reels from the short-form batch. Carousels get saved/shared in this
    niche ("10 SOPs every agency needs" performs as a save-magnet).

### Communities (week 2+, high intent, zero cost)

- r/n8n, r/gohighlevel, r/Entrepreneur (value posts only), the GHL and
  automation Facebook groups. **Read each group's self-promo rules first.**
  Formula: post a genuinely useful teardown (e.g., the reply-scoring prompt
  logic) → mention the kit once at the end or in comments when asked.
- List Tier 2 on **Gumroad Discover** as a second storefront (10% fee but free
  marketplace traffic; reuse the same zip + `content/SALES-COPY.md`).

### Week 2 — Volume + communities (≈6 hrs)

- Daily video (5–11), daily X post, thread #2, carousels #2–3.
- Two community value-posts (r/n8n + one FB group) using the teardown formula.
- Ask the first buyers (even if it's 2 people) one question by email: "what
  almost stopped you from buying?" — that's next week's objection content.
- Weekly review: identify the 1–2 videos with outlier watch-time/CTR.

### Week 3 — Double down on winners (≈6 hrs)

- Remake the two winning videos with new hooks (same body). Daily cadence
  continues (videos 12–18, thread #3, carousels #4–5).
- Two more community posts (different groups).
- Add a "Toolkit" link to the site's main nav/footer.
- If email list ≥ 25: send a mid-sequence broadcast (the objection email works).

### Week 4 — Paid test on proven creative (≈5 hrs + ad budget)

- Keep organic cadence (videos 19–25, thread #4, carousel #6).
- **Paid test, $300 cap for the month:**
  - Take the best organic video → TikTok Promote/Spark Ads at $15/day AND the
    best static ad → Meta Advantage+ at $15/day, both to /toolkit with UTMs.
  - Kill rules: pause any variant at $75 spend with zero checkout-page events,
    or CPC > $1.50 sustained. One $297 sale pays for the whole failed test.
  - Ad copy variants are in `creatives/static-ad-prompts.md` §ad-copy.
- Day 30: full review — see §6 targets — and decide: scale paid to $30/day,
  stay organic-only, or fix the funnel (page CTR fine but no sales = page
  problem; no clicks = hook problem).

## 5. Weekly operating rhythm (after day 30)

Monday batch (2 hrs) → daily post via scheduler (Buffer/Later free tier, 10
min/day) → Friday 15-min review. Roughly 3 hrs/week to keep the engine warm;
the email sequence, storefronts, and evergreen SEO page sell passively.

paid scaled to winners. If < 30%, the positioning/page needs work before more
traffic — book a session to rework the hero and hooks against the data.
