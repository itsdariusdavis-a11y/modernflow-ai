Design: brand system (near-black #0a0f0d, emerald→teal→cyan gradient accents,
Outfit headings, DM Sans body, glass cards). Build in Canva or generate
backgrounds with the kit's own static-ad pipeline. 1080×1350. Slide 1 is the
hook — big type, no logo clutter. Last slide is always the CTA:
**"Link in bio → modernflowai.com/toolkit"**.

Caption formula: first line = slide-1 hook rewritten, 2–3 sentences of context,
CTA, then hashtags: `#aiautomation #n8n #automationagency #gohighlevel
#smallbusinessautomation #agencyowner #digitalproducts` (rotate 5–7 per post).
