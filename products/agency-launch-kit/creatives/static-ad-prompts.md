Two uses: (1) organic carousel/feed images, (2) the week-4 Meta paid test.
Generate with the repo's own static-ad pipeline (`BRAND-AD-GENERATOR.md` /
`.claude/skills/static-ad-generator`) — and record the session as content
(video script V8).

## Brand DNA block (paste as the pipeline's brand-research output)

- **Brand:** ModernFlow AI — AI Agency Launch Kit
- **Palette:** near-black `#0a0f0d` base; gradient accents emerald `#10b981` →
  teal `#14b8a6` → cyan `#06b6d4`; glass-card surfaces `rgba(255,255,255,0.04)`
- **Type:** Outfit (headlines), DM Sans (body), JetBrains Mono for small
  `// LABEL` eyebrow text
- **Tone:** operator, anti-guru, artifact-proof. Never lifestyle imagery, no
  laptops-on-beaches, no money/lambo iconography, no income claims.
- **Visual motifs:** n8n node canvases, file trees, terminal/mono text, glass
  dashboard cards, subtle emerald glow lines on dark

## §ad-copy — Meta paid test (week 4)

Audience: Advantage+ with interest seeds (n8n, GoHighLevel, marketing
automation, agency owners). Objective: sales (pixel on /toolkit + Lemon
Squeezy checkout). $15/day, 3 creatives × 2 primary texts, kill rules per
MARKETING-PLAN §4-week-4.

**Primary text A (proof):**
"I run an AI automation agency for contractors. This kit is the actual
operating system: 10 SOPs, 4 importable n8n workflows, 8 prompt templates, and
the follow-up automation I deploy for clients. No videos. No community. Files
you own, from $97. 14-day refund."

**Primary text B (contrarian):**
"AI agency courses give you hours of video and zero working documents. This is
the opposite: the SOPs, workflows, and templates a real agency runs on —
one-time price, instant download, 14-day refund. Bring the execution."

**Headlines (rotate):** "The files, not the course." / "An agency's operating
system, $97+." / "Import the workflows today."

**TikTok Spark Ads:** promote the best-performing organic video as-is (Spark
preserves the native look); same budget and kill rules. Don't run TikTok
traffic to a cold static — native video outperforms there.

## Compliance checklist (every creative, paid or organic)

- [ ] No income promises or implied earnings anywhere (image or caption)
- [ ] Only real prices and real product contents shown
- [ ] Any dashboard/screenshot uses demo data — never client PII
- [ ] Refund policy stated accurately (14 days)
- [ ] Paid posts marked as ads by the platform (automatic) — never boost from
      a personal profile pretending to be organic
