# BRAND DNA DOCUMENT

==================

Sources: live site copy + design tokens pulled directly from the modernflowai.com codebase (`client/src/index.css`, `Hero.tsx`, `Services.tsx`, `Stats.tsx`, `Results.tsx`, `Pricing.tsx`, `About.tsx`), plus external web research (2026-07-03).

## BRAND OVERVIEW

- **Name:** ModernFlow AI (modernflowai.com)
- **Tagline / Hero:** "Stop Losing Leads. Start Closing Jobs." — section label motif: `// Automation Beyond Limits`
- **Design Agency:** In-house (founders Darius & Ryan, San Diego, CA)
- **Voice Adjectives [5]:** Direct, results-driven, confident, blue-collar-friendly, tech-forward
- **Positioning:** AI-powered automation systems for service businesses and contractors — capture, follow up, and convert every lead so owners "never miss another opportunity." Not a SaaS tool; a done-for-you partner ("We build...", "Systems built in 2–3 weeks").
- **Competitive Differentiation:** vs. platforms like GoHighLevel/QuoteIQ/NextPhone (self-serve software, generic SMB look), ModernFlow AI is a boutique done-for-you operator with a premium dark-mode tech aesthetic aimed squarely at trades (electric, plumbing, HVAC, roofing, landscaping, GC). Proof-first messaging: real contractor testimonials with hard numbers.

## VISUAL SYSTEM

- **Primary Font:** Outfit (geometric sans) — bold/700 headlines, tight tracking, leading 1.1
- **Secondary Font:** DM Sans (body), JetBrains Mono for `//`-prefixed section labels (code-comment motif)
- **Primary Color:** Emerald green `#00C896` (hard-coded brand green; Tailwind token emerald-500 `#10B981` = oklch(0.696 0.17 162.48))
- **Secondary Color:** Deep emerald `#00A67E` (gradient midpoint)
- **Accent Color:** Cyan `#00B4D8` (secondary highlight, alternates with green in UI feeds)
- **Background Colors:** Near-black with green tint `#0B120E` (oklch 0.145 0.014 155.83); cards `#121A16` (oklch 0.18); glassmorphism panels with emerald borders at ~12% opacity
- **Text Colors:** Off-white `#E7EBE9` headlines, muted sage-gray `#7A8580` supporting text
- **CTA Color and Style:** Emerald→cyan gradient pills ("Book a Free Strategy Call →"), rounded ~12px, plus outline ghost variant; gradient text treatment on key headline words

## PHOTOGRAPHY DIRECTION

- **Lighting:** Dark, moody environments with emerald/cyan glow accents; screens as light sources
- **Color Grading:** Deep blacks, desaturated cool shadows, vivid emerald highlights
- **Composition:** Split hero layouts — copy left, live UI widget right; generous spacing, grid-based
- **Subject Matter:** Working contractors (electricians, plumbers, HVAC techs, roofers) + dark-mode dashboard UI, phones showing SMS follow-ups, live automation feeds
- **Props and Surfaces:** Job sites, work trucks, tool belts, phones/tablets/laptops running the dark dashboard; glass cards floating over dark backgrounds
- **Mood:** Always-on, machine-working-while-you-sleep, confident momentum

## AD CREATIVE STYLE

- **Typical formats:** Stat-led proof ads, testimonial cards with real contractor results, before/after (missed calls → booked jobs), live-feed UI screenshots, dark headline ads with gradient text
- **Text overlay style:** Big bold Outfit headlines in white with one emerald/gradient phrase; mono `//` eyebrow labels; numbers oversized
- **Photo vs illustration:** Photography + real UI mockups; no cartoon illustration
- **UGC usage:** Contractor testimonials with name, company, city, and a hard metric
- **Offer presentation:** "Book a Free Strategy Call" (low-friction consult), setup + monthly pricing tiers
