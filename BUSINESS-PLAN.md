# ModernFlow AI — Business Plan

**Goal: $3,000/month in recurring revenue (MRR) within 30 days.**
**12-month target: $10,000/month MRR.**

> ⚠️ **Definition that drives everything in this plan:** the monthly targets are **pure monthly recurring revenue**. One-time setup fees are **excluded** from the goal — they're tracked separately as *cash collected* and exist to fund operations, not to count toward the $3k.

This plan is rendered interactively in the **Command Center app** (`planner/index.html`, deployed at `/plan/` on GitHub Pages) — work from there day to day; this document is the reference.

---

## 1. Executive Summary

ModernFlow AI is a **done-for-you AI automation agency** for local service businesses — contractors, plumbers, HVAC, electricians, roofers, landscapers. We install the systems that stop them losing jobs: instant AI lead response, smart CRM pipelines, automated follow-up and booking, and reputation management — built on GoHighLevel, managed entirely by us.

- **Who we sell to:** owner-operated service businesses doing $50k–$500k/year that miss calls and lose leads daily.
- **What they pay:** $97–$697/month recurring, plus a one-time setup fee.
- **The 30-day goal:** $3,000 MRR — roughly **6–10 paying clients** depending on tier mix.
- **The team:** Darius (developer / delivery) and Ryan (sales). Zero employees, near-zero overhead, ~80–90% gross margin.

## 2. Offer & Pricing

### Current tiers

| Tier | Setup (one-time, NOT counted toward goal) | Monthly (MRR — counts toward goal) | What they get |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Starter** | $1,000 | **$97/mo** | 3-page website, lead capture form, basic SEO, missed-call text-back |
| **Growth** ⭐ | $2,000 | **$297/mo** | 5-page website, booking system, payments, automated review requests, full follow-up sequences |
| **Elite** | $3,000 | **$697/mo** | 10+ pages, AI voice agent, AI chatbot, ongoing SEO, priority support |

### Recommended: the MRR-first "Founding Client" offer (first 10 clients)

The $3k goal is **MRR**, and big setup fees slow down closes without moving the goal. For the first 10 clients, restructure:

- **Growth+ Founding Offer: $497/mo, setup fee waived** (or $500 token setup), **3-month minimum**, in exchange for a testimonial + case study rights.
- Why it works: removes the $2,000 upfront objection (faster closes), each close adds **$497 MRR instead of $297**, and the 3-month minimum protects against quick churn.
- **At $497/mo, the goal is just 6 closes.** ($497 × 6 = $2,982 ≈ $3k — one Starter on top clears it.)

### Foot-in-the-door lead offer

**Free 15-minute "Missed Lead Audit":** we call their business after hours, text their listed number, and fill out their website form — then show them on a strategy call exactly how many leads they're losing and what it costs them. Costs us nothing, demonstrates the problem viscerally, and books calls.

## 3. Target Market & Positioning

- **The pain:** a missed call is a lost job. 60%+ of calls to small service businesses go unanswered; most never call back. They're on a roof or under a sink — not by the phone.

- **Why done-for-you wins:** these owners don't want software — they've tried apps and abandoned them. We set up, run, and maintain everything. They just see more booked jobs.
- **Positioning line:** *"Stop losing leads. Start closing jobs."*

## 4. The $3k MRR Math

### Client-count paths (current pricing)

| Path | Mix | MRR |
|---|---|---|
| A | 10 × Growth ($297) | $2,970 |
| B | 4 × Elite ($697) + 1 × Growth ($297) | $3,085 |
| C | 2 × Elite + 5 × Growth + 2 × Starter | $3,073 |
| **D (recommended)** | **6 × Growth+ Founding ($497) + 1 × Starter ($97)** | **$3,079** |

### Funnel assumptions (cold outreach to local service businesses)

| Stage | Conversion | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Outreach touch → real conversation | ~15% | call connect + DM/SMS reply combined |
| Conversation → strategy call booked | ~30% | the Missed Lead Audit hook |
| Call booked → call held | ~70% | confirmations + reminder texts cut no-shows |
| Call held → closed | ~30% | done-for-you + founding offer |

**Net: roughly 1 close per 100 touches.**

### Required daily activity (working backwards from 7 closes)

- 7 closes ≈ **700 touches over ~22 working days ≈ 30–40 touches/day**.
- **Ryan: 25–35 touches/day** (calls, DMs, walk-ins). **Darius: 5–10/day** (FB groups, supplier counters, warm network) plus all delivery.
- That throughput → ~15 conversations/week → ~5 calls booked/week → ~3.5 held → **~1–2 closes/week** from week 2 on.

The scoreboard in the Command Center tracks exactly these numbers daily.

## 5. The 30-Day Sprint

### Week 1 — Foundation (Days 1–7)
- Pick the niche + service area (one metro, one trade).
- Build the **lead list of 150** businesses (Google Maps, Yelp, FB local groups, supplier boards): name, owner if findable, phone, email, current website state, review count.
- Finalize scripts (Section 7), set up the GHL agency pipeline + Calendly link.
- Verify the ModernFlow site, Google Business Profile, and a 1-page founding-offer PDF are live.
- Build one **demo automation** (missed-call text-back on a test number) to show live on calls.
- Soft-launch: 20 warm-network messages each ("who do you know that runs a trade business?").

### Week 2 — Outreach Blitz (Days 8–14)
- Full outreach cadence: 30–40 touches/day combined (cold calls 9–11am & 4–6pm, SMS/DM mid-day, 2 in-person stops/week at supply houses or job sites).
- Every touch logged in the Command Center pipeline.
- Follow-up cadence on every conversation: Day 0 value message → Day 2 bump → Day 5 case/proof → Day 10 last call.
- Target: **10+ strategy calls booked** by end of week 2.
- Run the Missed Lead Audit before every booked call.

### Week 3 — Close & Deliver (Days 15–21)
- Hold strategy calls; present audit findings; pitch the founding offer; close on the call where possible (3-month minimum, card on file via GHL).
- **Target: first 2–3 closes.** Darius onboards each within 48h (Section 8) and launches inside 7 days.
- Capture the first before/after numbers (calls answered, leads responded to in <5 min).
- Keep outreach volume at full rate — closing is not a reason to stop prospecting.

### Week 4 — Double Down (Days 22–30)
- Referral ask to every new client at launch: "who are two other owners like you?" (offer a free month per closed referral).
- Publish the first mini case study; add it to outreach messages.
- Push to **6–8 total closes / $3k MRR** by day 30; book the overflow calls into month 2.
- Friday day-30 review: MRR, cash collected, funnel conversion vs. assumptions; set month-2 targets.

## 7. Sales Playbook

### Cold call opener
> "Hey, is this [Name]? This is Ryan with ModernFlow — real quick, I'm not selling anything on this call. We work with [trade]s around [city], and we ran a quick check: when people call your business after hours or while you're on a job, what happens to those calls? … That's actually why I called — we ran a test and [X] of your channels never responded. Want me to show you what that's costing you? Takes 15 minutes."

### SMS / DM
> "Hey [Name] — Ryan here, local company. Quick heads up: I tried reaching [Business] [yesterday] as a customer would and never got a response. That's jobs walking to the next guy on Google. We fix exactly that for [trade]s — instant text-back, automated follow-up, more booked jobs. Worth a 15-min look at what you're missing? No charge for the audit."

### Email
> **Subject: tried to hire you, couldn't reach you**
> Hi [Name] — I reached out to [Business] like a customer would and got no response within a day. Most [trade] owners we test lose 3–5 jobs a month this way — that's $2–10k walking away.
> We're ModernFlow AI. We install a system that answers every lead in under 5 minutes, follows up automatically, and books the job — fully done for you.
> Want the free 15-minute audit of where your leads leak? Two slots this week: [link].

### Follow-up cadence (after any conversation)
1. **Day 0:** recap + one specific finding from their audit.
2. **Day 2:** bump — "any thoughts?" + one-line value reminder.
3. **Day 5:** proof — case study / before-after numbers.
4. **Day 10:** takeaway — "closing founding spots this month, want yours?"

### Strategy call agenda (20 min)
1. Their numbers: jobs/month, average ticket, where leads come from (5 min).
2. Audit reveal: what we found when we tried to reach them; math on lost jobs (5 min).
3. Live demo: missed-call text-back firing in real time (5 min).
4. Founding offer + close: $497/mo, no setup, 3-month minimum, live in 7 days. Card on file today (5 min).

### Objection handling
- **"Too expensive"** → "One saved job pays for 1–3 months. The audit showed you're losing more than that *every month* right now."
- **"I already have a website/CRM"** → "Great — this isn't a website. It's the response system behind it. Your site collected the lead; nobody answered it in 5 minutes. That's the gap."
- **"I need to think about it"** → "Totally fair. What specifically — the money or whether it'll work? … The founding offer has 10 spots; [N] left. The 3-month minimum is exactly so you can judge it on results."
- **"I'm not techy"** → "That's the point — you never touch it. We build it, run it, maintain it. You just see more booked jobs in your calendar."

## 11. Risks & Contingencies

- **Week-2 reply rates low (<10%):** switch channel before switching message (calls ↔ walk-ins ↔ DMs); if still flat by day 14, switch niche within the trades list — the offer stays the same.
- **No-shows:** confirmation text at booking, reminder 1h before, "running late?" text at +5 min; no-show → instant rebook link, max 2 attempts.
- **Slow A2P/phone registration:** start registration on day 1 of onboarding; use email + FB Messenger automations until SMS clears.
- **Scope creep:** everything outside the tier sheet is a quoted add-on. "Great idea — that's a custom add-on, I'll price it" is the only sentence needed.
- **Churn risk:** the monthly numbers email is non-negotiable — clients cancel what they can't see working.
- **One of us gets pulled away:** the Command Center holds the full pipeline + playbook, so either person can cover the other's lane for a week.

## 13. Scaling to $10k/Month MRR in 12 Months

> Same definition as the 30-day goal: **$10k/month in pure recurring revenue — setup fees excluded** (tracked separately as cash collected that funds operations).

### Why low overhead makes this realistic
Each new client adds ~$100–700 of *recurring* revenue at near-zero marginal cost — overhead stays ≈$300/mo while MRR grows. Growth is constrained only by **sales activity** and **delivery hours**, both quantified below. There's no inventory, no ad budget required, no office.

### The MRR math at $10k

| Mix | Clients | MRR |
|---|---|---|
| All Growth ($297) | ~34 | $10,098 |
| Realistic mix: 8 Elite + 12 Growth + 10 Starter | 30 | $10,110 |
| **Restructured: ~20 clients @ $497/mo standard** | **20** | **$9,940 + Starters** |

The MRR-first restructure cuts the required client count by a third — that's the lever to pull early.

### Quarter-by-quarter

**Q1 (months 1–3) — Prove it: $0 → $5k MRR**
- Founder-led sales (this plan), 6–10 clients by day 30, ~15 by month 3.
- Systemize delivery into per-tier GHL snapshots; launch time ≤6h/client.
- Collect testimonials + 2–3 case studies with hard numbers.

**Q2 (months 4–6) — Referral engine: $5k → $6.5k MRR**
- Referral program live with every client (free month per closed referral).
- End founding pricing; new clients at $497+ standard monthly.
- Churn defense: monthly numbers email automated, quarterly check-in calls calendared.
- Capacity check: ~20 clients × 1.5h/mo maintenance ≈ 30h/mo on delivery — still fine for Darius.

**Q3 (months 7–9) — One leveraged channel: $6.5k → $8k MRR**
- Add exactly one scalable acquisition channel and feed it the case studies: local SEO/content ("plumber marketing [city]"), partnerships (supply houses, trade associations, BNI), or paid ads running our own product as the demo.
- First overhead hire: a VA for onboarding/admin (~$500–1k/mo) — frees Darius for builds, Ryan for sales.
- Begin annual-prepay option (10% off) to pull cash forward without touching MRR definition.

**Q4 (months 10–12) — $10k MRR**
- ~20–30 active clients depending on mix.
- **Churn assumption baked in:** at 5%/mo, a 25-client base loses ~1–2 clients/month — the targets above already require closing ~3/month net of churn.
- Capacity math: 25 clients × 1.5h ≈ 38h/mo maintenance + ~4 new launches × 6h = ~60h/mo delivery. Decision point: hire a part-time builder vs. automate harder. Rule: hire only when MRR covers the hire 3× over.
- Year-end: $10k MRR ≈ $120k ARR run-rate at ~85% margin, with setup-fee cash on top.
